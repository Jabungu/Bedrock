About

The purpose of this platform is to be a resource for people in times of crisis, such as blackouts, outages, and/or cut off water supply.It is to make communication easier, data more accessible, and information regarding resources readily available.

WHEN

It was designed during the severe winter storm during February 2021 that left millions of Texas residents in single digit cold weather without heating, electricity, and water. The goal was to alleviate communication challenges between people in crisis seeking to understand the circumstances, their available resources, and ways to help one another.

WHY

During the winter storm, a few things stood out that contributed to the ideation of this platform. One, the lack of clear communication between Texas grid operators, and the consumers. The consumers were left to figure it out with each other with little to no transparency from their providers. It was inpsiring to see Texas residents rally together in support of one another during such a tough time.

Purpose

Created by developer Job Oderoh, the platform exists to make crisis communication easier and more transparent, as well as make data and information on resources more acessible during a crisis.
